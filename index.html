About Me

I am a Physics MS student at California State University, Northridge studying condensed matter. I am slated to graduate in May, 2025 and am currently applying for PhD programs in Materials Science/Engineering and Applied Physics. My career aspirations are to earn my PhD and work in Research and Development for novel materials, particularly those for military/DOD end use or applications in extreme environments. Someday I would love to work at JPL or for Nokia Bell Labs!
