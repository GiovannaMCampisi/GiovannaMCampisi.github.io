Yazdani Lab, Joseph Henty Laboratories, Princeton University

Summer PREM REU Student Researcher - June 2024 - August 2024

I was awarded a position as a REU student researcher for the PREM Center at the MRSEC at Princeton University, working with Dr. Ali Yazdani and his High-Resolution Scanning Tunneling Microscopy (STM) laboratory. As a summer research student in Dr. Yazdani's Lab, I primarily worked on sample construction and design, fabricating a patterned gate monolayer graphene device designed to trap quasiparticles/Anyons in nanopores for imaging. The process involved manually exfoliating monolayer and multilayer graphene and hBN, cleaning and categorizing these materials using Optical Microscopy and the AFM, designing and etching a patterned gate in the AFM using electrode free local anodic excitation (EFLAO), stacking the device on a transfer state and cleaning/processing the device for installation.
